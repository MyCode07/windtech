WT-1000

The WT-1000 features a generous rotor diameter of 100 meters, allowing it to efficiently capture the energy from the wind. With a hub height of 120 meters, it reaches higher wind speeds, further enhancing its performance.

The WT-1000 is our flagship wind turbine, designed specifically for large-scale projects. With a remarkable power output of up to 2.5 megawatts, it is capable of producing a substantial amount of clean and renewable energy.

With its exceptional power output and robust design, the WT-1000 is a reliable and efficient choice for large-scale wind energy projects, contributing to a greener and more sustainable future.
